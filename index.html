What We do

We believe that mentors play a crucial role in our lives and we want to expand mentorship access to students everywhere.

  • Webinars

    Weekly career and college-readiness webinars for all parties involved in our web platform.

  • Mentor Video Calls

    Embark on video calls with mentors through our app.

  • Mentor Search

    Personalized search to find your mentor by location, major, university, or company.

MentorRoom is expanding educational support beyond schools and classrooms for students. We are connecting high school students with college mentors, and college undergrads to professionals through our mobile app and online platform. Our product will allow mentors and students to not only connect, but actually engage with each other by embarking on video conference calls through our app. By uniting 9th-12th graders, university students, and professionals, we aim to reduce the achievement gap in the U.S and help students have access to mentors and advice through the click of a button.


After logging in to our app, students can search by location, university, or company to find a mentor, and swipe up or down through mentor profiles until the find the right one. Then they can select the mentor they would like to speak to for advice by swiping right to jump on a video call. We believe that mentors play a crucial role in our lives and we want to expand mentorship access to students everywhere. We will also have weekly career and college-readiness webinars for all parties involved.


Essentially, we are creating an online community of mentors from around the nation to inspire, inform, and prepare more students to attend a university and pursue their dreams, and help undergrads engage with professionals in the industry of their interest to pursue their passions. We not only wish to connect people, we want to go beyond and allow people to engage with each other and create global communities of support.
